Bio

Theodore is a labrador-retriever mix from a local shelter. He came to us at 6mos old. Young Theodore was all legs and nerves when he first arrived, but over the years, this boy has grown to be a complete and total lovable snob. His family loves him very much, and despite not always wanting to accept our hugs and affection, we know he loves us, too.
